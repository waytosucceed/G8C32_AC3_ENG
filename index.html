Words that I Learnt from the Story

Shimmering: Reflecting a gentle light that seems to move slightly, or attractive.
Example: Stars were shimmering in a cloudless sky.

Alchemist: A person who transforms or creates something through a seemingly magical process.
Example: One of his owners was an alchemist, whose secrets he seems to have learnt.

Plenty: An adequate or more than adequate number or amount of something.
Example: She had plenty of time to finish the task.
